METHOD USED


a. Extraction of Total Genomic DNA

The total genomic DNA was extracted from the given sample using CTAB/NaCl, Phenol Chloroform method standardised by PAR Life Sciences and Research Pvt Ltd, Trichy. Quality of the genomic DNA was assessed using 0.7 % agarose gel along. The quantity of the genomic DNA was assessed in UV-Vis Spectrometer (Labman). Quality of the DNA was good enough to proceed to PCR.


b. RBCL gene amplification

Amplification of matK gene was carried out for the samples using universal primers and himedia 2X master mix (forward primer: ACCCAGTCCATCTGGAAATCTTGGTTC Reverse primer: CGTACAGTACTTTTGTGTTTACGAG) with standard reaction volume. With following PCR condition intial denaturation with 95°C for 5 min followed by 40 cycles of 94°C for 30 sec, 56°C for 40 sec as annealing temperature and 72°C for 30 sec as cyclic extension followed by 10 min of 72°C as final extension in Himedia Insta96plus thermal cycler. Amplion size ~ 900bp (fig 1) was observed in all the samples (Fig. 2). PCR-generated amplicon were purified using GeneJET PCR purification kit (Thermo Scientific, EU-Lithuania) to remove the primer dimer and other carryover contaminations. The quality of the product was assessed using 1.8% agarose gel along with 100bp DNA ladder as size standard and the product was found to be good for sequencing.
